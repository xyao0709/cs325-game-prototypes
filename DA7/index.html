Animal House

  • Originality: Game origin is from a tutorial for making Phaser 2 game .
  • Technical Merit: This game use mouse to control the basket to move left or right to catch and avoid objects.
  • Prototype Postmortem: For this assignment, I spent almost all of my time on understanding what each instruction mean, and know most of their meanings. That's the hardest part for me. However, changes made onto that game is easy since I only replace some image and audio before class beginning which is caused by that I don't have enough time to make changes on it.

  • High Concept: Use mouse to catch more cats and avoid catch dog.
  • Theme: People with lots, lots and lots cat.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: direct mouse Randomnees: random Genre: relax Play Styles: joker
  • Prototype Goal: Move mouse into indicated place to catch items, and the ability to figure out the items in moving.
  • Player Experience Goals: Has the ability to use mouse.
  • Gameplay: Players use mouse to control basket to move left or right to catch or avoid objects, and they could buy desired objects at shops.
  • Strategies: Be careful of the falling staffs and avoid indicated items. They will also need to arrange appropriate house for animals.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: There are many people who want to keep pets, but the life prevent them. Here is a good place to keep cartoon pets virtually. In this house, staffs hold shop and collecting spot to help people get the nacessary objects to buy desired animals.
  • Target Audience: People who loves cute things or want to be relaxed from games.
  • Play Time: Each round can be play through a few seconds to minutes, and the whole game can go through seconds to minutes.
