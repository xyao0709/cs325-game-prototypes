Catch Cats

  • Originality: Game origin is from , which is a tutorial for making Phaser 2 game.
  • Technical Merit: This game use mouse to control the basket to move left or right to catch cats and avoid dogs.
  • Prototype Postmortem: For this assignment, I spent almost all of my time on understanding what each instruction mean, and know most of their meanings. That's the hardest part for me. However, changes made onto that game is easy since I only replace some image and audio before class beginning which is caused by that I don't have enough time to make changes on it.
  • Prototype Assets: All of sources are borrowed online, and were modifies to remove the original background and cut into small pieces.
    • Images: Pinterest homepage
    • Audio: Background Music
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Code is borrowed from a Phaser 2 tutorial.
  • High Concept: Use mouse to catch more cats and avoid catch dog.
  • Theme: People with lots, lots and lots cat.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: direct mouse Randomnees: random Genre: relax Play Styles: joker
  • Prototype Goal: Move mouse into indicated place to catch items, and the ability to figure out the items in moving.
  • Player Experience Goals: Has the ability to use mouse.
  • Gameplay: Player use mouse to control basket to move left or right to catch cats, and avoid catch dogs. The prototype is catch as many items as possible without time limit.
  • Strategies: Just be careful of the falling staffs and avoid indicated item (the dog).
  • Story/Setting/Premise: There was a girl who wants to keep cats, but her parents didn't allow her to do this. One night, when she fell in sleep with desire of cats, she would find she was in a strange place where cats would fall from the sky. She didn't want cats to be injured, and she found there was a basket under her feet. She use the basket to catch cats and daily supplement to raise her cats in her dream. The story is irrelevant with the gameplay strategies, but the story can guide players to play.
  • Target Audience: People who loves cute things or want to be relaxed from game are targeted audience.
  • Play Time: It can be play through a few seconds to minutes. This doesn't have time limit.
